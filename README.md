# classic-poems

Chinese classic poems through the years — a personal anthology arranged by date.

This repository collects my modern **old-style Chinese poems (舊體詩)**, organized by year. I will **add my historical poems gradually** over time.

## Files

- `2023.md` — Poems written in 2023
- `2025.md` — Poems written in 2025
- `2026.md` — Poems written in 2026 (ongoing)
- `千問推薦.md` — A curated “selected works” (2011–2026) with thematic organization and commentary

## Themes (from the currently included selections)

- Reflections on society and the times (感世、憂世)
- Personal experience, travel, and diaspora/sojourning (羈旅、鄉愁)
- Family life and teaching/encouraging the next generation (教子、祝願)
- Historical reflection and classical allusions (詠史、用典)
